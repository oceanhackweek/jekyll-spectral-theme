ABOUT OCEAN HACKWEEK

The Ocean Hackweek is a 5-day learning hackathon geared towards democratizing data access and increasing exposure to technological assets developed as part of the Ocean Observatories Initiative (OOI). The hackweek will be held at the University of Washington from Aug. 20 - Aug. 24 2018 with goals of facilitating processes that accelerate research, promoting collaboration and cultivating data science literacy among the scientific community served by the OOI.
